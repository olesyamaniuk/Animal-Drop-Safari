About the Game

"Animal Drop Safari" is a colorful match-3 game that brings you to the heart of the wild.

Players complete tasks, meet adorable animals, and explore exotic locations.

Game Highlights

  • Tropical Locations

    Travel through jungles, deserts, savannas, and even volcanoes.

  • Animal Helpers

    Each animal has a unique ability to help you complete levels.

  • Souvenir Collection

    Gather souvenirs from each level to build your safari album.

Unique Game Features

  • Animal Superpowers

    Unlock animal abilities to overcome challenging levels.

  • Global Map

    Discover new continents with exotic animals and landscapes.

  • Exploration

    Find hidden locations and uncover safari secrets.

  • Vibrant World

    Every detail of the game is inspired by wildlife and its inhabitants.

Main Characters

  • Lola, the Baby Elephant

    Brave and friendly, she helps break blocks.

  • Ziggy, the Funny Zebra

    Rewards you with extra moves for your successes.

  • Max, the Little Lion

    Unleashes energy that destroys everything around.

How to Play

  • Match Elements

    Combine identical symbols to create combinations.

  • Use Hints

    Apply animal abilities to complete challenging levels.

  • Explore

    Unlock new territories by completing missions.
